Save the Forests

You have been summoned by "Conservation International" to save the Indo-Burma forests located in the Indo-Chinese subregion. The forests cover Cambodia, Lao PDR, Myanmar, Thailand, Vietnam, and parts of southern China. They are considered one of the richest biodiversed locations in the world because of its diverse ecosystem composition, including mixed wet and dry evergreen forests, deciduous and montane forests, lowland floodplains, swamps, and mangroves. The greatest threat to its biodiversity is habitat loss, primarily caused through deforestation due to commercial logging and agriculture. Your mission is to stop the loggers...

Instructions

  1. Use the arrow keys to move the player
  2. Press the spacebar to shoot loving forces
  3. Press "B" to launch special love attack
  4. Collect the peach to boost your health or sunflower to boost your special power
  5. Beware of poisonous fruits, they will decrease your health
